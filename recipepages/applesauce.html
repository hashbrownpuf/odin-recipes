Applesauce Recipe

Detailed Description Below

There’s nothing like a batch of homemade applesauce! Our homemade applesauce recipe is so good, it’s a revelation. Use fresh apples and add seasonings to make it your own.

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds apples
  • 2 stips lemon peel
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Boil Apples
  2. Remove lemon peels, and mash the apples
  3. Enjoy!
